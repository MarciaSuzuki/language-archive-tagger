# Language Archive Stories - English Recording Script
## 30 Minutes of Audio Content (~600 segments)

**Recording Instructions for Eleven Labs:**
- Voice: West African English accent
- Pace: Natural storytelling rhythm, clear pronunciation
- Style: Warm, narrative tone
- Pauses: Brief pause between sentences
- Output: One MP3 per story, or one MP3 per sentence
